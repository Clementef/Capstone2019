What is project name?

Project name is a project made for Oakland Tech's 2019 Capstone class by Clemente Ferrer.

What is Money?

Against all logic, money on this website is invaluable. You get 10 currency per week and can give it to whoever you want for whatever reason you want. Both the sender and receiver get an equal amount of reputation for a transaction.

What is Reputation?

You get one reputation for every currency you send or recieve. If you send your friend 10 currency, you both get 10 reputation. Reputation has real value and can be redeemed.

In other words, reputation = money given + money received
